Coding Challenge

Instead of doing interveiws, we want to gauge commitment level along with talent and so we have decided to have a week-long coding challenge to determine the suitable candidates for our club. The final project does not have to be perfect, so don't be intimidated, instead, we want to see your approach to developing the given project. We would also be holding office hours throughout the week with experienced Hack4Impact engineers so that you can ask questions and learn from them.
